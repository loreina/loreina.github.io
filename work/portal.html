Portal: U.S. Immigration
Application Assistant

Type of work
Product & visual design

Tools
Sketch, Origami Studio

Date
February 17-19, 2017

Portal is an immigration application assistant project that started at Treehacks. In light of recent U.S. executive orders in early 2017, we wanted to build a product to alleviate the immigration process as USCIS applications are often confusing and full of legal terminology.

The idea was to create a basic-language questionnaire app that would sort and transmit the collected survey data to output as a USCIS application form. I was responsible for the product and visual design of the project.
