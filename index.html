about me

Hello there! My name's Loreina and I'm an undergraduate student at McGill University in Montreal. I love to design, watch/make films, and am passionate about using design to create a more sustainable future.

I'm the co-founder and president of Project Play, a student-led non-profit organization focused on using creative mediums to empower youth, educate, and promote social change.

I currently work at 7hourdev, and as a freelance designer. Previously, I've worked at Prime Leaders of York Region, and the Law Office of Metz L. Ngan.
