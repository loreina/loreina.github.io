about

Hello there! I'm Loreina, an arts student at McGill University in Montreal. I love to design, listen to podcasts, watch movies, and learn new things. I care a lot about good design and exploring its intersection with technology, entrepreneurship, and sustainability.

I work at 7hourdev on front-end design and web development. I'm also the co-founder of Project Play, a student-led non-profit organization about creativity, education, and social change. Previously, I've worked at Prime Leaders of York Region, and the Law Office of Metz L. Ngan.

Please scroll to learn about some of the work I've done. You may also want to get in touch.
