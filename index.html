about me

Hello there! I'm Loreina, an Urban Studies student at McGill University. I love to design, listen to podcasts, watch movies, and learn new things. I care a lot about good design and exploring its intersection with technology, entrepreneurship, and sustainability.

I work at 7hourdev on front-end design and web development. I'm also the co-founder of Project Play, a student-led non-profit organization about creativity, education, and social change. Previously, I've worked at Prime Leaders of York Region, and the Law Office of Metz L. Ngan.
