Hi there, I'm Loreina. I'm a second-year student studying international development and communications at McGill University in Montreal. I like movies, architecture, and building cool stuff from ideas.

I lead the design for McHacks, Canada's most innovative hackathon. Previously, I've worked at Project Play, Prime Leaders of York Region, and the Law Office of Metz L. Ngan.

I love exploring the intersection between design and technology through product innovation, interaction design, user experience, and more. I'm eager to tackle design systems thinking challenges and find effective, user-friendly solutions.

Currently, I am learning how to be a great product designer, working on design prototyping and systems thinking to build useful and impactful products.
