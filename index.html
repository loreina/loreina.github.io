Hi there, I'm Loreina. I'm a sophomore at McGill University studying international development and urban systems. I like to design, watch movies and build cool stuff from ideas.

I am the director of design for McHacks. Previously, I've worked at Project Play, Prime Leaders of York Region, and the Law Office of Metz L. Ngan.

My work is motivated by the social and technological impact of architectural spaces. I'm passionate about exploring the intersection between design and architecture to create a more human-environment interactive future.

Currently, I am learning how to be a great product designer, working on design prototyping and systems thinking to build useful and impactful products.
