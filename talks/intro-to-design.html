I gave my first talk and workshop in October 2017 for HackMcGill, the student society at McGill University responsible for McHacks.

It covered several design fundamentals including: colour, type, space, grid, and design thinking. We also looked at some case studies and examples of good web design such as Alphabet, Spotify, and Airbnb. It ended in a quick workshop on building iOS and Android interfaces using Figma.

By no means am I a "professional" designer, I'm still learning everyday and picking up new tricks through podcasts, books, and of course, the internet. My frame of mind is that learning design is like learning to play an instrument: start with the basics, keep practicing, and never stop practicing.
